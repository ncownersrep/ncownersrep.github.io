Meet the Team
Nathan Cheng,

Principal

Nathan began working in construction 26 years ago, remodeling homes in the East bay hills. In 1995, he began developing commercial projects, including his own retail stores, restaurants, and bars. In 2002, he started a management consulting business, working with private companies, as well as several public agencies and community organizations. He has managed commercial projects of varying budget and scope, and is highly skilled at coordinating and overseeing multiple subcontractors and consultants simultaneously. His experience in a wide array of settings over the years have made him an adroit and personable project manager with a proven track record of success.

Alex Young
Alex is a proud native of the San Francisco – Bay Area. While completing his degree in City Planning at the University of California, San Diego, he decided to focus his skill set on Project Management and Business Process Improvements (BPI). Since then, Alex has worked in a wide-range of business environments to improve and optimize the underlying operations in achieving project goals. His diverse experience in managing different projects has allowed him to adapt to many business situations. His attention to detail coupled with his interpersonal skills makes him a proficient and skillful member on any team.

Daryl Soe
